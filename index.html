How to Get There

The wedding is being hosted at the Quazza family home: Poursay, Torxé.

The Church is Église St Martin de Tonnay-Boutonne,
a 30 minute walk from the venue.

By Plane

The closest airports to the wedding venue are Poitiers (PIS) and La Rochelle (LRH) however the most frequent flights from the UK go to Bordeaux (BOD).

From there, we would recommend renting a car (via Sixt) or do a Blablacar carpool to get to the destination.

If you want to car pool / share the drive with other guests, fill in this car pool form once you've booked your flights and we'll put you in touch with people flying in / out at similar times.

By Train

The closest train station to the wedding venue is Surgères, closely followed by Saint Jean d’Angely (book via SNCF connect). There are direct trains from Paris to Surgères. If you need help getting from the train station to the venue, we’re happy to help!

Useful tip: you can get the equivalent of a railcard (Carte Avantage Adulte) which gives you and a +1 30% discount off your trip.

By Car

Google maps knows the places (Poursay, Torxé and Église Saint Martin).

Note - there’s another Poursay that’s about 30mins drive out so make sure to include Torxé in the search.

Where to Stay

You can find places to stay on the standard Booking.com and Airbnb. We also suggest having a look at GreenGo and Gîtes de France .

Note: On the night of the wedding, you are welcome to pitch a tent at the venue in a designated location.
