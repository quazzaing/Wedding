How to Get There

The wedding is being hosted at the Quazza family home: Poursay, Torxé.

The Church is Eglise St Martin de Tonnay-Boutonne.

Note: the church is a 30 mins walk from the wedding venue. If you find an accommodation in Tonnay Boutonne or Torxé, you won’t need a car during the wedding day.

By Car

Google maps knows the places (see links above) - ‘Poursay, Torxé’ (note - there’s another Poursay that’s about 30mins drive out so make sure to include Torxé in the search).

By Train

The closest train station to the wedding venue is Surgères, closely followed by Saint Jean d’Angely (book via SNCF connect). There are direct trains from Paris to Surgères. If you need help getting from the train station to the venue, we’re happy to help! Useful tip: you can get the equivalent of a railcard (Carte Avantage Adulte) which gives you and a +1 30% discount off your trip.

By Plane

The closest airports to the wedding venue are Poitiers (PIS) and La Rochelle (LRH) however the most frequent flights from the UK go to Bordeaux (BOD) (via Skyscanner). From there, we would recommend renting a car (via Kayak) or car pool (via Blablacar) to get to the destination.
